About us

Our team of economists is currently working on an app that can calculate the amount of carbon emissions you produce. It is crucial to reduce greenhouse gas emissions from transportation by transitioning to modes of transport with lower emissions, such as public transport, cycling, and walking. Hence, we have started developing the carbon emission app to facilitate this transition.

Estimated launch date: April
